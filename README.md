# Car Sales Analysis Insights:

The Car Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across car models, salespersons, and customer purchase behavior. It highlights key financial metrics such as total sales, commissions, car model trends, and annual performance insights.

**Analyst:** Leo  
**Date:** 26/07/2024  

---

## Problem Statement:
The car dealership industry relies heavily on understanding sales trends, top-performing salespersons, and customer purchase behavior to maximize profit and improve marketing strategies. However, without clear visibility into total sales, commissions, and model performance, dealerships struggle to make data-driven decisions.  
This dashboard was created to help identify performance gaps, evaluate sales effectiveness, and uncover insights that guide better sales planning and resource allocation.

# Key Findings:

- **Strong Sales Year:** Over $31B in total sales.  
- **Top Salespersons:** A small group contributes heavily to revenue.  
- **Best-Selling Models:** Altima, Civic, Silverado show consistent high performance.

- **Commission Variance:** Some models yield higher commission opportunities.

---

# Recommendations:

### **1. Boost High-Performing Salespersons**
- Provide incentives, bonuses, and leadership opportunities.  
- Encourage them to mentor lower-performing staff.

### **2. Focus Marketing on High-Performing Car Models**
- Prioritize Altima, Civic, and Silverado for promotions.  
- Consider improved financing options to boost sales of underperforming models.

### **3. Improve Customer Targeting**
- Use customer purchase behavior to run personalized campaigns.  
- Align commission structures to encourage profitable sales.
